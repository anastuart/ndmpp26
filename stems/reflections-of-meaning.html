Values & What Matters
Exploring distress through the lens of unmet or threatened values

Somatic & Embodied Access
Using body awareness to bypass cognitive defences and access felt experience
01Where do you feel this in your body right now — as we're speaking?
02If you drop out of the thinking for a moment — what does your body know about this?
03Can you stay with that sensation for a moment? What comes up when you let yourself feel it?
04When you describe x — what happens in your body? Does something tighten, or sink, or contract?
05If that feeling in your chest/throat/stomach could speak — what would it say?
06What does your body want to do with this feeling — move, contract, protect, reach out?
