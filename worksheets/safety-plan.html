This safety plan is a personal guide to help me stay safe when I am feeling overwhelmed or having thoughts of suicide. I will keep this plan somewhere easy to access (phone, wallet, notebook).

My Warning Signs
These are thoughts, feelings, behaviours, or situations that tell me I may be heading into a difficult period.
Examples: feeling hopeless, withdrawing from others, trouble sleeping, intense anxiety, thinking "people would be better off without me."
